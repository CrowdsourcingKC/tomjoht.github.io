Flare's stylesheet editor is very powerful but can be intimidating at first, so my stylesheet's purpose is to help people get comfortable with the editor. This stylesheet, and a document that explains how to use it and discusses styles in general, are available at www.hyperword.com/FlareStylesheet.htm

I've played around with Flare's style editor for a while and have found it to be one of the most robust style editors I've seen.
