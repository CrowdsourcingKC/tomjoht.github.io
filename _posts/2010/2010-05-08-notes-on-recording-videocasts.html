10 things about video

* 1. lighting
* 2. external mic: binaural mics
* 3. a hook/story. first videocast discussion the best
* 4. editing video really hard, so hit record only when ready
* 5. where to look
* 6. length. short, so cut right to the issue.
* 7. format: 720p, 30 fps. mov format. m4v format for ipod.
* 8. captions and transcription
* 9. need for a camera person
* 10. video is really fun -- didn't think i would like it, but loved it. video is incredibly easy.
* 11. need multiple people in the video -- not just one person.
* 12. scheduling people ahead of time versus doing it all spontaneous.
