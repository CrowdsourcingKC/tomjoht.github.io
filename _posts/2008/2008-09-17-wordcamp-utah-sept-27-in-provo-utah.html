Wordcamp Utah will be held Sept 27, 2008 in Provo (at Novell). If you’re a blogger living in Utah and you’re interested in immersing yourself in a full day of WordPress presentations, conversations, and other exchanges, be sure to attend. See the Wordcamp Utah site for more details. Cost is $20 per person and you need to register.

A looooong time ago I attended a planning meeting for this Wordcamp. Joseph Scott is the main organizer for the event. Thom Allen will be speaking on podcasting, and Cameron Moll will also be presenting.
