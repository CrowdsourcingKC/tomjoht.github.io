Content re-use

  • DITA: Conditional profiling
    You can add attributes to elements and then filter your transforms to include any element that has attributes with certain values.
  • Conref (re-use of content)
    You can re-use notes and other content through an attribute called conref.
  • DITA: Ditaval files (filtering attributes from your transforms)
    Ditaval files define conditional processing rules for your transforms.
